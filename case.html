Real Cases & Statistics 2025

There are some statistics of phishing, malware and man-in-the-middle attacks in Hong Kong since 2025.

1. Phishing Attacks

7,800 cases reported in 2025 (5-year high)

Primary Targets: Banking, Finance and Electronic Payment.

We can see that there are significant threats for e-banking systems and users in recent years. There is an urgent need for enhanced security measures to protect e-banking users from these threats.

2. Malware Surge

480% increase in Trojan-based attacks in 2024.

Common Tactics: Trojans targeting electronic devices disguised as legitimate applications.

It may pose several risks to electronic devices for e-banking. This rise in malware emphasizes the importance of vigilance and robust security protocols within e-banking platforms.

3. MITM (Man-in-the-Middle) Attacks

Top Risks:

  • Public WiFi hotspots in cafes/airports.
  • Unverified SSL certificates in banking apps.

These trends highlight the critical need for continuous security assessments and improvements within e-banking systems to safeguard against evolving cyber threats.

⚠️ PROTECT YOURSELF: Always verify sender details and use official banking apps!
